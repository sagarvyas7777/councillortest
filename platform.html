Platform

What we'll fight for.

A focused plan for our ward — built from thousands of doorstep conversations.

🏠

Attainable Housing

  • Permit more "missing middle" homes near transit
  • Stronger renter protections at the municipal level
  • Partnerships with non-profits to build family-sized units
🛡️

Safer Streets

  • Traffic calming on school routes
  • Better lighting in parks and laneways
  • Fund community-based mental health response
🌳

Parks & Green Space

  • Protect the mature tree canopy
  • Renew aging playgrounds
  • Expand the river trail network
🚌

Reliable Transit

  • On-time, frequent bus service
  • Protected bike lanes that connect
  • Plan infrastructure for the city we're becoming
