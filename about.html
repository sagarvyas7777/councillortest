About

Meet Harinder.

Harinder grew up in our ward, went to school here, and now raises a family on the same block where they learned to ride a bike.

A decade of community work

From co-founding a neighbourhood tenant association to chairing the local Business Improvement Area, Harinder has spent ten years bringing people together around practical, local solutions.

Why run for council?

Because our ward deserves a councillor who shows up — at the doors, at the meetings, and at City Hall. Someone who reports back honestly and listens before deciding.

Outside of work

You'll find Harinder coaching the under-11 soccer team, volunteering at the community kitchen, or walking Maple (the dog) along the river trail.
